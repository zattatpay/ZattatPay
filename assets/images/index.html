Payment Gateway Solutions

What is Online Payment Gateway?

An Online Payment Gateway (PG) is a secure online payment processing solution that enables businesses, corporates, and government entities to process online transactions made through debit (ATM) cards. By providing a robust payment gateway, businesses can ensure that online payments made through both the internet and mobile apps are secure. The Zattat Gateway accepts all locally issued debit cards in Bahrain and can be customized to fit seamlessly into your business model.

Features

  • The Zattat Gateway offers secure and real-time transaction processing through a customizable platform that follows a three-step security process. The process begins by checking for validity and encrypting transaction details, followed by ensuring that the transaction is sent to the correct destination, and finally decrypting the response and sending it back to the shopping cart, billing, or account system. The Gateway seamlessly integrates with various shopping cart software, databases, internet merchant accounts, and protocol exchange servers, providing a flexible payment solution.
    • Please note that for credit card processing, you will need to contact credit card payment gateway acquirers.
